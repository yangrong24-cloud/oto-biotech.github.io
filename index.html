OTO Biotech

Engineering next-generation biological solutions.

We develop innovative technologies at the intersection of molecular biology, data, and engineering to address unmet challenges in biotechnology.

The Problem

Biological systems are complex, costly to engineer, and difficult to scale using conventional approaches. Existing tools often lack precision, robustness, or translational efficiency.

Our Solution

OTO Biotech builds platform technologies that enable more reliable, scalable, and programmable biological systems. Our approach integrates advanced molecular design, quantitative experimentation, and data-driven optimization.

Technology & Focus

Our work spans early-stage research through applied development, with current focus areas including novel biological engineering strategies, tool development for improved system control, and translational pathways toward industrial and therapeutic use.

Team

OTO Biotech is led by scientists and engineers with training in chemical engineering, biological systems, and quantitative modeling. We collaborate closely with academic and industry partners.
